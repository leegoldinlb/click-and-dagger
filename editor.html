SECTOR HEIGHT

Dial a FLOOR and/or CEILING height, then RAISE/LOWER to brush cells or FILL to flood a whole room. Eye sits 0.5 above the floor; walls block steps taller than 0.5. RIGHT-CLICK with a height tool reverts a cell to its material default.

TEXTURES

Pick a TARGET, click a texture, then paint cells to retexture their floor/wall or ceiling — independent of height and material. RIGHT-CLICK reverts a cell's texture. A cyan-bordered corner marks a custom ceiling.

SECTORS

This is a vector map. Click (or SPACE) drops wall vertices; close the loop on the first point to make a sector. I inserts a vertex on a wall. DEL removes; Ctrl-Z undo. Pick a person/prop below then click to place it. Set floor/ceiling/textures/sky/win/doors in 3D PREVIEW.
